Research Paper on George Floyd

The document explores the profound impact of George Floyd's death, emphasizing the role of Darnella Frazier's recording of the incident and its viral spread on social media. It describes Floyd's tragic death at the hands of a police officer, sparking widespread protests and outrage worldwide, despite the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. The aftermath included both peaceful demonstrations and violent riots, drawing attention to systemic racism and police brutality. Additionally, the movement spurred by Floyd's death led to increased support for Black-owned businesses and initiatives promoting diversity and equality in various sectors.
